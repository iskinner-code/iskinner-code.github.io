Website Evaluations

Intel
  • Contrast: Everthing on the site constrats well with one another, the colors mix well and don't clash. The text is easil readable due to the color.
  • Repetition: Font styles and color schemes are consistant, maybe changing which color is applied in the background but using the same colors for the sites page.
  • Alignment: Text and images are aligned to fit each section of the site very well and allow for great transitions. It makes the site easily readable and easily traversable.
  • Proximity: Everything is grouped together very well, such as new products and what they can do, then news about the company, and finally additional information such as downloads and company details.

Pacific Northweast X-Ray Inc.
  • Contrast: While parts of the site are easy to read, some parts have lighter/neon colors which clash with the background, making it to read or look at. Some images are different sizes than others, either too big or too small
  • Repetition: Font size tend to remain the same, however some parts of the website will have different colors and switch from green, to yellow, and then to black.
  • Alignment: Sometimes the text and images are not aligned properly. In parts of the website, there will be an image right after some text, causing a wide gap in the page. Also, text will be misaligned due to where an image is placed, causing the text to look cluttery at points.
  • Proximity: In places proximity seems good and everything seems somewhat organized, in other places however, it can feel very packed or jammed together. Besides that proximity is good.
